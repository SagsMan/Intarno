Intarno WEBSITE PROJECT BRIEF 
Luxury Furniture, Interior Design & E-Commerce 
Platform 
PROJECT VISION 
Intarno's website should function as more than an online store. 
The platform should position Intarno as a premium furniture and interior design brand 
capable of serving residential, commercial, and international clients through a seamless 
digital experience. 
The website should combine: 
● Luxury Brand Positioning 
● Interior Design Expertise 
● Furniture Manufacturing Excellence 
● E-Commerce Functionality 
● Project Showcase 
● Consultation & Lead Generation 
The experience should feel like a premium architectural and interior design publication while 
maintaining the functionality of a modern e-commerce platform. 
DESIGN CONCEPT 
The Curated Canvas 
The website should feel like a luxury editorial magazine. 
Design principles include: 
● Large immersive photography 
● Generous white space 
● Elegant typography 
● Minimalist layouts 
● Smooth transitions 
● Architectural precision 
● Premium storytelling 
The goal is to make visitors feel as though they are exploring a world-class furniture 
showroom rather than browsing a conventional online store.

DAILY STANDUP FORMAT (15 MINUTES) 
Every day, ask the developer: 
1. What was completed yesterday? 
2. What are you working on today? 
3. What is blocking progress? 
4. What do you need from us? 
Then ask: 
Are we still on track for Day 14 launch? 
If the answer is "No," immediately identify the blocker and assign an owner.
